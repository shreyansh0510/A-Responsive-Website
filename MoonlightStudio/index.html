VISION

We love to tell your story !

Moonlight Studio is a production and management company designed for an ever-changing media landscape. For our clients, we deliver personalized service and best-in-class visuals. For our artists, we develop, nurture, and maintain lasting careers.
Moonlight was founded in 2006 by Jen Jenkins. Jen began her career in music, managing bands and in the process crossing paths with the photographers assigned to shoot their publicity images and album art. A creative herself, she saw book potential in many of these photographers’ work and went on to spearhead a number of seminal music monographs.
